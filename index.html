Overview

Connor is a tenacious computational biologist interested in applying machine learning to analyze genomics and single-cell transcriptomics data.

Connor's work combines the use of DNA-based genomics large-language-models, single-cell foundation models and agentic systems to generate testable biological hypotheses.

With previous experineces including being awarded access to the UK's national Isambard-AI supercomputing facility, working as a Research Fellow at the Alan Turing Insitute, and achieving 1st author publications in Nature Communications, Connor will not rest until the power of AI is fully harnessed towards biolgical sciences.
